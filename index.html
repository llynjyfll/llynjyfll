Frequently Asked Questions

How long do your flowers typically stay fresh?

Our flowers typically stay fresh for 7-14 days with proper care. We provide care instructions with every delivery to help you extend the life of your blooms.

Do you offer same-day delivery?

Yes, we offer same-day delivery for orders placed before 12 PM local time, Monday through Saturday, depending on availability and delivery location.

Can I customize a bouquet?

Absolutely! We love creating custom arrangements. Please contact us directly to discuss your preferences and special requests.

What if the recipient isn't home for delivery?

If the recipient isn't available, our delivery person will either leave the flowers with a neighbor, in a safe place, or attempt redelivery the next day, depending on your preference specified at checkout.
